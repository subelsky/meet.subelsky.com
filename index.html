Where?

I am usually in the Canton neighborhood of Baltimore about once a week, typically on Mondays. Otherwise I am working out of my home office in Charles Village.

Here is where I prefer to meet in Charles Village in descending order of preference:

  • Walking or jogging around the neighborhood (starting at my house and walking up the Greenway through the beautiful Guilford neighborhood)
  • Walking around the BMA sculpture garden
  • Lunch or coffee at Donna's
  • Lunch or beer at Charles Village Pub
  • Lunch at Pete's Grill

When?

If you are up for a walk or a jog my schedule is very flexible as I do those things almost every weekday. I don't run fast; I just want to get my heart rate elevated. I also walk and run in all seasons and weather conditions. You can also just talk to me over the phone while I run. If either of these options work for you please email me one or two days in advance and we can lock down a particular time.
